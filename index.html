Our Delivery

Download and choose a dish

Find the Healthy Food app in the App store or Play Market of your phone and download it. Then choose a dish in the application and place an order.

Cooking

After confirming the order, the dish is prepared by our chef. It will take some time depending on the number of selected dishes. If you cook 1-3 dishes cooking time up to half an hour, if more than 3 dishes then up to an hour cooking time.

Order processing

Your order has been received and is being processed by our employee. After a while he will call you to confirm the order. Wait please
